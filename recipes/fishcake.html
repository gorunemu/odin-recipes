Fishcake

Description

This is a popular side dish, eaten with rice. It can be made sweet or spicy, depending on your taste. It's a fast and easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • fish cake, sliced into thin pieces
  • rice syrup
  • toasted sesame oil
  • toasted sesame seeds
  • medium onion
  • carrot, sliced thinly
  • garlic clove, minced
  • green onion, chopped
  • hot pepper paste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat sliced fish cakes until light golden brown
  2. Add onion, carrot, and garlic and keep stirring until onion is translucent
  3. Turn heat down and add rice syrup and optional hot pepper paste to fishcake and vegetables mix
  4. Remove from heat. Add green onion, toasted sesame oil and sprinkle sesame seeds onto mix
  5. Enjoy with rice
