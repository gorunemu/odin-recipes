Tofu Soup

Description

Tofu soup is a spicy soft tofu soup filled with soft tofu, vegetables and meat or seafood of your choice. It can be eaten with rice. It is a yummy comfort food, perfect for those wintry snowy nights. This is a simple recipe that uses chicken stock instead of typical anchovy stock.

Ingredients

  • soon tofu
  • egg
  • garlic
  • green onion
  • white onion
  • kimchi
  • korean chili powder
  • meat or seafood
  • chicken stock
  • cooked rice

Instructions

  1. Chop up garlic, white onion, and green onion
  2. Heat up garlic and onion in pan
  3. Add meat and cook it
  4. Add kimchi and then add chicken stock/broth
  5. Add soon tofu and break it up gently
  6. Add korean chili powder to taste
  7. Crack egg over top and let it cook
  8. Sprinkle chopped green onion and serve with rice
