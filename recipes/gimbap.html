Gimbap

Description

Gimbap is like Korean sushi. It has vegetables, meat and rice, just about everything that's heavenly delicious! It might take a lot of prep work but it's insanely worth it.

Ingredients

  • sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • cooked rice
  • ham, cut into strips
  • imitation crabmeat
  • carrots
  • spinach
  • yellow pickled radish
  • eggs
  • toasted sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Mix toasted sesame oil with cooked rice
  2. Boil water and blanch spinach
  3. Cut carrot into matchsticks and saute it until soft
  4. Cut radish into strips
  5. Heat up ham strips and imitation crab meat
  6. Cook eggs and cut into strips
  7. Lay out nori sheet, spread rice over 1/3 top of sheet and then lay out ingredients on lower 2/3 of sheet
  8. Roll the nori sheet, pressing tightly until it's completely rolled all the way
  9. Repeat until out of ingredients
  10. Slice the gimbap into slices or eat it whole
